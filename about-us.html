ABOUT US

Whether you like chicken, beef, seafood, or vegetarian our extensive menu will have something for everyone.

We have sit down dining for those who wish to relax have a cup of tea and eat some great food. We also have take out for those on the go.
Party trays (multiple orders in a to-go tray) are also available to large parties on the go. Delivery is available for large orders by special request.

Our kind waiters and waitresses will be happy to take your order on the phone or in person.
Reservations for larger parties are welcome.
